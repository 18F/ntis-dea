How it works

Joint ventures are business partnerships for data-focused projects. Our partners are selected based on merit and include universities, nonprofits, and for-profit organizations. By partnering with the private sector, we improve the ability to government to better provide data and services in efficient means back to the public. We expect that public-private partnerships towards new data platforms and services will lead to next generation standards for serving up data for years to come.

We'll help your agency determine if the Joint Venture Program is right for you.
