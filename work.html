Our work

Building a new bridge between government and private sector innovation.

We solve problems by connecting government and companies to collaborate more efficiently and effectively to build next-generation solutions that can't be found anywhere else. One way we do this is through our Joint Venture Partners program.

NTIS Joint Venture Partners Capabilities

New Technologies

New technical capability or private sets to help statistical agencies derive economic, demographic and social insights from Federal and private sector data.

Better Public Services

Assistance through the integration of public and private data, the “Internet of Things,” cloud computing and predictive analytics, to derive insights for land use planning, and delivering improved public services
