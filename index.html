We believe federal data is the future of America

Our team partners with private-sector companies and organizations to help federal agencies with their data science needs.

Our Mission: Provide innovative data services to Federal agencies, through joint venture partnerships with the private sector, to advance Federal data priorities, promote economic growth, and enable operational excellence.

Our Vision: NTIS will be a center of excellence that delivers trusted data networks through agile partnerships with the private sector and enables the private sector to develop and/or use new and improved data products and services.

Our Scope: NTIS will support the entire data delivery pipeline for creating unique platforms to access, analyze, and use data; combining and using data in new ways to enable innovative products and services; and delivering better data services to businesses, communities, and citizens.
