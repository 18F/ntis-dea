We’re building a new bridge between the government and private sector

How we work

One of the government’s largest untapped resources is data. Agencies produce extraordinary amounts of data, but they don’t always know what to do with it.

When a government agency needs help analyzing, publishing, or managing its data, we use a special authority—Joint Venture Authority— (JV Authority) to pair them with experts from the private sector. That means agencies work side-by-side with universities, nonprofits, and private-industry professionals. Together, they solve problems and build solutions.

Partner with us

Government agencies

Put your data to good use. We can connect you with other federal and private-sector data providers. Focus on your mission and let us handle the infrastructure. We’ll pair you with experts who will bring insight and innovation to your project. They’ll help you create new products and services. All we need to get started is an interagency agreement or a memorandum of understanding.

Private sector

Our partners are leaders in education, industry, and nonprofits who partner with the federal agencies we serve. Our partners have opportunities to create new tools, new ways of working with data, and new solutions for complex, meaningful data challenges.

The process

Decide you want to work with us. → Identify the challenge. → Sign an agreement. → Create great things. Together.
