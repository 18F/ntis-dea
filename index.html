How we work

Government agencies produce extraordinary amounts of data, but they don’t always know what to do with it.

When a government agency needs help analyzing, publishing, or managing its data, we use a special authority (Joint Venture Authority) to pair them with experts from the private sector. That means agencies work side-by-side with universities, nonprofits, and private-industry professionals. Together, they solve problems and build solutions.
