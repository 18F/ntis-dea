We believe federal data is the future of America

Our team partners with private-sector companies and organizations to help federal agencies with their data science needs.

Our mission: We provide innovative data services to federal agencies, through joint venture partnerships with the private sector, to advance federal data priorities, promote economic growth, and enable operational excellence.

Our vision: We will be a center of excellence that delivers trusted data networks through agile partnerships with the private sector and enables the private sector to develop and/or use new and improved data products and services.

Our scope: We support the entire data delivery pipeline for creating unique platforms to access, analyze, and use data. We combine and use data in new ways to enable innovative products and services. We deliver better data services to businesses, communities, and the public.
