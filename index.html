We partner with private-sector companies and organizations to help federal agencies with their data science needs.

Joint Venture Partners program

Our Joint Venture Partners program gives NTIS unparalleled speed-to-market capabilities, allowing entrepreneurial partners and innovators to build new tools and find new ways to use government data to solve challenging problems.

Agile data services partner

Our mission is to provide innovative data services to Federal agencies, through joint venture partnerships with the private sector, to advance federal data priorities, promote economic growth, and enable operational excellence. We are a center of excellence that delivers trusted data networks through agile partnerships with the private sector and enables the private sector to develop and/or use new and improved data products and services.

End-to-end data pipeline

NTIS supports the entire data delivery pipeline for creating unique platforms to access, analyze, and use data; combining and using data in new ways to enable innovative products and services; and delivering better data services to businesses, communities, and the public.
