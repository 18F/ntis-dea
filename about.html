About Us

The Service was originally established in the Department of Commerce as the Publication Board in the wake of World War II. It's mission was to make available to industry the huge amounts of scientific research that had either been commissioned by the Government or captured from the enemy.

Since 1965, NTIS has focused on research produced for and by federal agencies.

Since June 2015, NTIS' mission is focused on expanding access to the government's data resources, within an emphasis on data concerning the nation's economy, population, and environment. NTIS:

  • deliver government data services through agile partnerships with the private sector
  • enable the private sector to develop new and improved data products and services
  • support trusted data networks with stringent privacy and security.
